AL-EMAN ISLAMIC CENTRE OF CANADA
Al-Eman Islamic Centre of Canada was established in 2005. The centre is conveniently located in downtown Mississauga and offers Daily Prayers, Friday Prayers, Quran classes, Marriage Services and much more to serve the Community in Mississauga.

PLEASE READ CAREFULLY BEFORE YOU COME TO THE CENTRE:

  1. Registration for Friday prayers is must because of limited space.
  2. The Musalah opens 15 minutes before Iqama and closes 15 minutes after the prayer is done.
  3. Due to limited capacity Seniors over 60 are not allowed.
  4. Due to limited capacity kids under 10 are not allowed
  5. No place for sisters to pray
  6. Washroom & Wudhu facilities will NOT be available. Please come with your wudhu.
  7. No one is allowed to wait outside the Mussalla.
  8. Anyone experiencing symptoms must not attend (fever, runny nose, coughing, sneezing, aches, etc).
  9. Anyone who has recently been in close contact with someone with Covid-19 must not attend.
  10. Free parking available.
  11. No cellphones allowed to be used inside the centre.
